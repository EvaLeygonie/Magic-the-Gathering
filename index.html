Introduction

Welcome to the World of Magic: The Gathering!

Magic: The Gathering is a fantasy-themed collectible card game created in 1993 by Richard Garfield and published by Wizards of the Coast. It combines strategy, creativity, and skill as players take on the role of powerful wizards called "Planeswalkers," summoning creatures, casting spells, and battling for victory across the Multiverse. With thousands of unique cards and endless gameplay possibilities, Magic offers something for every kind of player, whether you're a casual gamer or a competitive strategist. Dive into the cards, explore the lore, and discover your own path to greatness!
